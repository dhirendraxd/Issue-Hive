IssueHive — AI Context File

Product identity:
IssueHive is a student-first issue reporting and campus community platform built for colleges in Nepal.
It centralizes campus problem reporting, public discussion, and resolution tracking so student voices,
faculty, and administrators can collaborate transparently.

High-level purpose:
- Reduce fragmented reporting across chats, emails, and informal channels.
- Create a single public place to report and follow campus issues.
- Improve accountability through visible status updates and resolution history.
- Strengthen trust through community engagement and transparent moderation.

Who uses IssueHive:
- Students: report issues, vote, comment, follow updates, and message peers/admins.
- Faculty/Staff: monitor concerns and communicate progress.
- Administrators: prioritize recurring issues, resolve reports, and track campus patterns.

Key user journeys:
1) Sign in (email/password or Google).
2) Report an issue with title, details, category, urgency, and optional anonymous mode.
3) Community validates issue via votes and comments.
4) Issue owner/admin updates progress and marks resolution.
5) Users track status changes and engagement over time.

Core feature areas:
- Campus issue reporting (public, transparent, structured).
- Anonymous issue posting option.
- Voting and comment threads for signal and discussion.
- Progress updates and resolution metadata.
- Reporting/moderation flows for unsafe or abusive content.
- Direct messaging and follow/follower social graph.
- Basic analytics and SEO-focused content structure.
